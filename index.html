Latest Project

Medical AI

A Blockchain-powered secure medical record system where patient data is stored using SHA-256 encryption and can never be tampered or deleted. Built with Node.js & Express on the backend, Claude AI predicts diseases by analyzing symptoms and vitals in real-time, giving differential diagnoses and treatment recommendations. Every medical record is mined as a block into an immutable chain — making healthcare data transparent, secure, and intelligent.

AgroAssist

AgroAssist is an AI-powered web portal that helps farmers detect crop diseases through photo analysis, get soil-based crop suggestions for all 38 Tamil Nadu districts, and plan seasonal cultivation based on climate and weather changes. The system features a multilingual AI assistant that communicates in Tamil, English, Hindi, and Telugu, providing real-time market prices, profit estimates, and personalized farming advice.
